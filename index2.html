Music Organizer

The inspiration for this website came from a desire to view all of the music I listen to in one place. The goal is to create a pleasant viewing experience that encourages artwork appreciation and organization of not just the music in my library, but old and new music that I have yet to purchase. There are no download links, just YouTube and BandCamp links to listen to everything for free. The open database I created allows for my friends and I to contribute our own music compositions as well as whatever we are listening to, to grow a massive collection for the community.

Create a free account!


View collection

This is where you can see all of the music in our database. You can search or sort by Artist, Release Date, or New Additions made by other users. This is available without an account and by far the most elegant music visualization tool on the web.

Favorites

If you see something in the collection that you like, you can simply add it to your favorites by clicking the star! You can view just your favorited music in the "" tab and sort by our various filters. This feature is only available with a free account, so sign up!

Add Albums

This is where you can add any album to our collection. This is one of our coolest features because anybody can contribute their own music or their favorite music to be seen by everyone! Be prepared to enter some meta-data when you submit, and if some is incorrect a live human will fix all the errors. This is also only availble when signed in.
